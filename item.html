FUR HAT

Beanie: 80% alpaca - 20% synthetic
Border: 100% alpaca huacaya fur

COLORS

SIZE

One size (20.5 cm / 8.07 in approximate diameter)

Description

Enjoy a cold day outside with a fashionable yet warm alpaca fur hat. Hand-crafted by a lovely family of artisans in Peru. The beanie is made of 80% alpaca / 20% synthetic wool and the edge is made of 100% alpaca fur.

Care Instructions

Do not wash or dry clean. To remove stains: In a bowl mix some warm water and mild soap or shampoo. Mix to create foam. Apply the foam with a cotton cloth over the stain, be careful not to saturate the fur with water. Dry the fur hat with a blow dryer or at room temperature and brush. For liquid spills: Rub the fur hat case with baby powder and brush after drying.
